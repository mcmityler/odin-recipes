Grilled Cheese Sandwhich Recipe

Home

Description

Tasty delicious grilled cheese sandwich recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 Slices of bread
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Salted Butter

Steps

  1. Thinly slice cheese or shred it. Enough to cover the slice of bread
  2. Butter one side of each slice of bread
  3. Put a slice of bread butter side down in a medium heat preheated pan
  4. Cover the slice of bread in the pan with cheese
  5. Put the second slice of bread on top of the cheese with the butter side facing away from the cheese
  6. Flip the sandwich after the slice is golden brown
  7. Cook second side until golden brown and then remove from heat and eat

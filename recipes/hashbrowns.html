Description

This is a quick and easy hashbrown recipe that takes about 10 mins of cooking time and 10 mins of prep.

Ingredients

  • 1-2 Russet Potato (prefferably)
  • 2tbps Neutral Cooking Oil
  • 2tps Salted Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Smoked Paprika (optional)
  • Garlic Powder (optional)
  • Onion Powder (optional)
  • Cayenne Pepper (optional)

Steps

  1. Peel Potatoes
  2. Grate Potatoes into a bowl of water (so they dont get discoloured)
  3. Rinse Potatoes in strainer until water runs clear
  4. Squeeze Potatoes Dry and lay out on towel
  5. Air dry potatoes for 5 mins
  6. Put oil and butter in pan on medium high heat until butter is slightly browned
  7. Add potatoes to the pan
  8. Cook potatoes on medium heat with a lid covering the pan for 5 mins
  9. Remove lid and season hashbrowns and then flip them
  10. Cook for 2-4 more minutes until otherside is golden brown
  11. Plate hashbrowns and enjoy

Peanut Butter Sandwhich Recipe

Home

Description

A great late night snack, here is a delicious peanut butter sandwich recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 Slices of bread
  • Peanut butter

Steps

  1. Spread peanut butter on one side of bread
  2. Put bread together into a sandwich with peanut butter in the middle
  3. Eat sandwich and enjoy!
